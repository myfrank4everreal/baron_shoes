Adidas Yeezy 350 v2 Carbon Review

The yeezy design team hasn't really varied too much on the box in fact they've kept things pretty much the same over the life of the 350 v2s the box is made up of natural cardboard with 350 printed on the top and boost printed on one of the sides the back or the front of the box depending on which way that you look at the box has a pull out drawer as most easy boxes do and then the other side of the box has a size tag which like i mentioned before still says asriel azrael azrael instead of carbon carbon carbon for the colorway is that a problem no not at all but it does indicate that the name of the sneaker was changed after the manufacturing process

jumping back into the sneaker itself as with most 350v2s a majority of the upper is made up of prime knit this time around the primeknit comes in a nice subtle mix of colors and the base color is of course black woven throughout the black primeknit you've got some really nice steel blue which is actually the only accent color on the medial side of the shoe on the lateral side of the shoe things do get a little bit crazier I mean as crazy as black primeknit patterns can be near the toe of the shoe the black is accented by a light cream color as you move farther back you've got some more of that steel blue and then just above that on sort of the top quarter panel of the shoe you've got a really interesting mix of steel blue and sort of a grayish green color that color placement actually adds sort of a second side stripe to the shoe which i'm into running down the center of the lateral side you've got your black side stripe and as usual because it's meshed you can sort of see through it however because it's not a clear plastic mesh it's much more difficult to see your sock color than with other colors of the shoe and then moving up on the shoe you get to these black rope laces which weave through the primeknit upper and also don't have ends. it's this sort of infinity loop lacing system which is kind of standard for new Yeezys.

I got to be honest with you the more that i wear Yeezys that have this lacing system the more that i like it i really love the fact that it's so much easier to tighten by just pulling on it and also it doesn't get in your way like you don't have this giant bow over the top of your shoe it never kind of dangles along the side it's just kind of there and i like that about it if you decide to switch out these laces the box does actually come with an extra set of laces in the same color except with ends and if you do decide to switch them out you are gonna have to cut the original laces which kind of sucks but there's really no other way to take them off the shoe.

Moving inside the shoe you've got a black sock liner with the adidas 3 stripes in a reflective black material then rounding off the inside of the shoe you've got a black insole with the adidas and Yeezy branding printed on the heel in stone blue when it comes to sizing and fit the adidas easy 350 v2 carbon fits just like every other pair of 350 v2s i mean you're probably gonna guess that already over the last few years since its launch i feel like adidas has slowly fixed the sizing problems with the 350 v2 so i wear this shoe true to size rather than going a half size up however if you like to go a half size up and 350 v2s because you're used to that you still can do that in this shoe as well however if you have the chance to try this shoe on before you buy it or any other pair of 350 v2s i guess newer pairs of 350v2s i would definitely suggest doing that to make sure you're grabbing the right size for you.

Then moving around to the heel of the shoe you'll notice that there's no heel tab on this pair of 350 v2s i have no idea why they decide to go with heel tabs on some shoes and they don't go with heel tabs on other shoes it doesn't really make sense to me but this one doesn't have one and that's fine moving down on the shoe you get this really nice color contrast between the black primeknit upper and the semi-translucent cream white midsole even though this colorway isn't anything crazy it's one of the more solid color ways and it still provides a really nice contrast because you've got a very light color on the bottom and a very dark color on the top this is the kind of Yeezy that you can wear every single day it's not an earth tone like every other pair of 350s and it's not neon green so it's really easy to rock and this shoe is also very easy to rock all day because of the full length boost midsole.

And then finally moving to the bottom of the shoe you've got this cream colored outsole which is slightly more yellow than the midsole the Yeezy 350 v2 carbon is a really solid Yeezy and it's one of the most wearable Yeezys because of this super clean black and cream makeup it's a simple colorway for sure but i think that's what makes this shoe so great.
